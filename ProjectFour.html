Knight and Mystery

Overview

The walk through of Level design which are divided into 5 divisions and searching for objective with added back story.
Design: I used sprite package from Unity Assets store and designed whole level using Tile Map.
Code: Added player keyboard inputs which trigger player animations, and created animations with sprite sheet of knight. Features like checkpoint, teleport and damage for player.
